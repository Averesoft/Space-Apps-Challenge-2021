## Ingenii Solis
### Space Apps Challenge 2021

This is team Ingenii Solis's submission to the 2021 NASA Space Apps Challenge! We are submitting to the "You Are My Sunshine" Challenge. 

This website will take in a Google Maps URl, which it will then convert to a latitude and longitude. It can also take a user's current position and use it as the latitude or longitude. It take these parameters and show a graphical display of the weekly average "available sunshine" using NASA's database.
